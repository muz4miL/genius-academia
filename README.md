## üìñ Overview

The **Edwardian Academy ERP** is a bespoke management solution designed to handle the complex financial flows of a multi-partner educational academy. Unlike generic LMS platforms, this system focuses on **Financial Integrity** and **Revenue Splitting**.

It features a strict **"End of Day Closing"** mechanism that acts like a digital bank vault‚Äîlocking floating cash collected by partners and converting it into verified revenue for the central ledger.

## ‚ú® Key Features

### üëë For the Owner (Executive View)
* **Central Command:** Real-time visibility of total collections (Chemistry, Physics, Pool).
* **Debt Tracking:** Automated tracking of "Recoverable Debt" (Expenses paid on behalf of partners).
* **Profit Sharing:** Instant calculation of the **40/30/30** profit split model.
* **Expense Management:** Record academy-wide expenses that automatically update partner debt ledgers.

### ü§ù For Partners (Faculty View)
* **Floating Cash Wallet:** Tracks cash collected from students in real-time.
* **Digital Vault:** One-click **"End of Day Closing"** to securely lock cash and transfer it to the admin.
* **Revenue Transparency:** See exactly how much tuition has been generated and verified.
* **Privacy:** Strict RBAC ensures partners see *only* their own financial data.

### üé® The "Royal Edwardian" UI
* **Glassmorphism Design:** Premium frosted glass cards with dynamic gradients.
* **Gold & Midnight Theme:** A bespoke color palette reflecting prestige and authority.
* **Interactive Elements:** Hover-lift effects, pulsing alerts, and smooth transitions.
